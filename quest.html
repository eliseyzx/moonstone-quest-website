How to play Moonstone Quest

Register, activate a digital heritage passport, follow the map, scan QR checkpoints, complete challenges, collect badges and redeem rewards.

Before you begin

  • Charge your phone.
  • Register and activate your passport.
  • Check the current theme and event dates.
  • Use public, accessible routes.

During the quest

  • Visit cultural nodes.
  • Scan each official QR marker.
  • Complete the location challenge.
  • Save each unlocked badge and story.

Complete the season

  • Collect the required stamps.
  • Reveal the full story chapter.
  • Present your passport to a listed partner.
  • Return for the next theme.

Community-powered

Residents contribute stories, businesses host checkpoints and redemptions, and project partners help review and improve each season.
